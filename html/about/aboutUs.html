Welcome to MCLS

The Madison Chinese Language School (MCLS) is a non-profit organization with its main objectives of teaching Chinese language, promoting Chinese culture and providing these related services to the general public. MCLS was formally organized in January 1997, and is a current member of the Chinese School Association in the United States (CSAUS).

MCLS offers variety of classes for both children and adults. Among them are reading, writing and conversation of the Chinese language, Chinese culture and history, enrichment mathematics, Web design and publishing, Chinese folk dance, drawing and martial arts. Pinyin system and simplified Chinese characters are used in Chinese language teaching. Classes are conducted on Sunday afternoons in accordance with the Spring, Summer and Fall semester schedules of UW-Madison.

MCLS is a not-for-profit organization administrated by a board of members. Most of them are parents. Except for teachers, all the administrative team members are unpaid. Parents' participation in school operations is strongly encouraged.

For more information about MCLS, please visit the school's web site at http://www.mcls-wisc.org, or email MCLS Board at board@mcls-wisc.org.
